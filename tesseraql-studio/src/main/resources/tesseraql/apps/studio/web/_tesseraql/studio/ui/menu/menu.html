Sidebar menu

The app's declarative sidebar (config/menu.yml), rendered into the shell and filtered per item by the caller's roles/permissions. An item with no roles and no permissions is public; otherwise it shows when the caller holds one of the listed roles or permissions. Edits are saved to config/menu.yml and take effect on the next reload or restart. With no items the app falls back to its hand-authored templates/nav.html.
